## What raises attractiveness across the board
Verified evidence depth (pillars, real PMIDs); proven organic traffic; an owned
newsletter; documented B2B relationships/leads; clean data provenance & IP; a
separable, exportable vertical (esp. Skin Finder / Connect); low founder
dependence (SOPs); a defensible IT-market position.

## Editorial-independence guardrail
Several buyer categories (manufacturers, retailers, pharmacies) create a
**conflict-of-interest risk** with the "independent, commission-doesn't-move-the-
grade" promise. Any deal must preserve editorial independence contractually or
the core asset (trust) erodes. `LEGAL REVIEW REQUIRED`.
