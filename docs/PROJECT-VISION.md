# Project Vision — Nucleo Longevity

> Single source of truth for what this project is, what it is not, and where it
> is going. If a proposed feature contradicts this document, the document wins
> until it is deliberately changed.

## One line
**Live longer, age better — inside and out.**
_Vivere più a lungo, invecchiare meglio — dentro e fuori._

## What it is
Nucleo Longevity is an **independent, evidence-graded longevity observatory**.
Every molecule — whether taken (systemic) or applied (topical) — receives an
**A–F grade based only on human clinical evidence**, never on marketing. Skin
longevity is treated as **one face of longevity**, not a separate brand or site.

Three pillars:

1. **Observatory (editorial).** Graded molecule entries, evidence hub, method
   pages. This is the trust asset and the SEO moat.
2. **Finder (utility).** A transparent price/offer comparator that turns
   editorial trust into a recurring reason to return, with clean affiliate
   economics and no scraping of prohibited sources.
3. **Connect (B2B).** A curated directory + digital fair linking the longevity
   ecosystem (manufacturers, labs, suppliers, distributors, services). Curated,
   not transactional — no accounts, no payments, no auto-publishing.

## What it is NOT
- **Not** a generalist wellness blog. Every claim is graded and sourced.
- **Not** a pure skincare brand. Skin longevity is a facet, not the whole.
- **Not** NUKLEO / NKF as a consumer brand name in the UI.
- **Not** a WordPress/WooCommerce build. It is a code-owned Next.js asset.
- **Not** a marketplace. Connect mediates introductions; it does not transact.
- **Not** a place that sells grades. Editorial independence is absolute:
  participation in Connect or Finder never buys, changes, or influences a grade.

## Audience
- **Primary:** evidence-curious consumers evaluating longevity/skin molecules.
- **Secondary:** professionals (formulators, distributors, sales reps, clinics)
  who value an independent, graded reference and a curated ecosystem directory.

## Strategic intent
Build a **defensible, transferable asset**: owned code + owned content + a
documented methodology + clean data room. Operate for cash flow (affiliate +
curated B2B advisory), architect for optionality (the whole thing can be handed
over, licensed, or sold without a founder in the loop).

_Build for value. Operate for cash flow. Architect for optionality._

## Non-negotiables
- **Editorial independence** from all commercial layers.
- **Evidence honesty:** unverified citations are labelled as such; no invented
  PMIDs; "sponsored" never implies "verified".
- **Clinical aesthetic:** hairline borders, mono labels, serif body, A–F chips;
  no gradients/glow/emoji/Inter.
- **EN-primary bilingual** (EN + IT) with correct hreflang/canonical.
- **No health-data collection** and no PII in redirect/affiliate flows.
