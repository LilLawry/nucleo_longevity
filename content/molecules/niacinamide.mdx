evidenceSummary: "Topical niacinamide has a reasonable body of controlled studies supporting improvements in skin-barrier function (reduced water loss), evenness of tone, sebum/shine and the look of enlarged pores, plus antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activity. Grade B for a broadly useful, well-tolerated active with consistent (if unspectacular) data."
