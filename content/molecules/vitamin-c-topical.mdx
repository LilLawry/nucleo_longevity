Unlike the oral form, the topical route can deliver meaningful concentrations to skin — but only if the product is well formulated: pure L-ascorbic acid is unstable and loses potency when oxidised (it turns yellow/brown). Grade B for a genuinely useful daytime antioxidant, with formulation as the deciding variable, distinct from any single product's claims."
