Human evidence is limited to a few small trials — for example modest, temporary cognitive improvement in older adults with mild impairment, and some mood/sleep signals — often small and short. There are no aging or neurodegenerative-disease outcome trials. Grade C: a genuinely interesting mechanism with an immature human evidence base."
